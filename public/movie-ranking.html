Paper Tigers

This indie film won a Sundance Award, and while it is not very well known, it is one of Cole's favorites. The film is an amazing coming of age film about those who were unwilling to face their demons in the past, but must at their current old age to change the trajectory of their current life. The movie is also an amazing representation of Asian-Americans in films!
